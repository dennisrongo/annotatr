How Alignment Guides Work

  • Move your mouse around the canvas to see alignment guides
  • Blue dashed lines appear when you align with center, edges, or existing shapes
  • The cursor automatically snaps to alignment positions (within threshold)
  • Click and drag to create shapes
  • Shapes will snap to alignments during drawing
  • Use the slider to adjust the snap threshold distance
  • Click "Add Test Shapes" to create shapes for alignment testing
